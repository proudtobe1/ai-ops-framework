# Use Case: Roadmap Alignment

A structured, AI‑assisted process for ensuring that team priorities, sprint plans, and long‑term initiatives remain aligned with the organizational roadmap.

---

## 1. Purpose

To maintain strategic alignment across teams by regularly reviewing roadmap items, dependencies, risks, and execution progress.

---

## 3. Trigger Conditions

Roadmap alignment is initiated when:

- A new quarter or planning cycle begins  
- Strategic priorities change  
- Major dependencies shift  
- AI detects priority drift  
- Leadership requests a roadmap review  

---

## 4. Process Overview

1. **Collect Roadmap Inputs**  
   - Strategic goals  
   - Quarterly objectives  
   - Team priorities  
   - Dependencies  
   - Risks  
   - Current progress  

2. **AI Summarization**  
   - AI generates a roadmap health summary  
   - AI identifies misalignment  
   - AI highlights risks and dependencies  
   - AI detects priority drift  

3. **Evaluate Alignment Using Decision Model**  
   - Compare roadmap items to team priorities  
   - Score alignment using system logic  
   - Identify gaps and conflicts  

4. **Adjust Priorities**  
   - Reassign ownership  
   - Re‑scope initiatives  
   - Update timelines  
   - Resolve cross‑team conflicts  

5. **Create Decision Record**  
   - Document alignment decisions  
   - Capture rationale  
   - Define next steps and success criteria  

6. **Communicate Updates**  
   - Share alignment summary  
   - Update stakeholders  
   - Adjust sprint plans if needed  

---

## 5. AI‑Assisted Steps

AI supports roadmap alignment by:

- Summarizing roadmap health  
- Detecting misalignment and drift  
- Highlighting cross‑team dependencies  
- Scoring alignment using system logic  
- Suggesting priority adjustments  
- Generating the decision record  

---

## 6. Output

A complete roadmap alignment includes:

- Alignment summary  
- Updated priorities  
- Adjusted timelines  
- Dependency updates  
- Decision record  
- Communication summary  

---

## 7. Success Criteria

Roadmap alignment is successful when:

- Priorities match strategic goals  
- Dependencies are clear and updated  
- Risks are identified and mitigated  
- Decision record is complete  
- Stakeholders are aligned  

---

## 8. Summary

This use case provides a structured, AI‑assisted method for maintaining roadmap alignment, ensuring teams stay focused on the highest‑impact work.
