# AI‑Ops Maturity Model

A structured model defining the stages of operational maturity as organizations adopt and scale the AI‑Ops Framework.

---

## 1. Purpose

To provide a clear, progressive roadmap for improving operational clarity, velocity, alignment, and AI‑assisted decision‑making across teams.

---

## 2. Maturity Levels Overview

The AI‑Ops Maturity Model includes five levels:

1. **Level 1 — Ad‑Hoc Operations**  
2. **Level 2 — Structured Operations**  
3. **Level 3 — Integrated AI‑Ops**  
4. **Level 4 — Predictive AI‑Ops**  
5. **Level 5 — Autonomous Operations**

Each level builds on the previous one.

---

## 3. Level 1 — Ad‑Hoc Operations

### Characteristics
- No consistent workflows  
- Unstructured communication  
- Decisions made reactively  
- Limited visibility across teams  

### AI‑Ops Indicators
- AI used inconsistently  
- No templates or system models  

### Goals to Advance
- Adopt basic workflows  
- Introduce templates  
- Establish operational cadence  

---

## 4. Level 2 — Structured Operations

### Characteristics
- Workflows documented  
- Templates used regularly  
- Basic operational cadence established  

### AI‑Ops Indicators
- AI used for summarization  
- AI‑assisted reporting begins  

### Goals to Advance
- Introduce system models  
- Standardize decision‑making  
- Improve cross‑team alignment  

---

## 5. Level 3 — Integrated AI‑Ops

### Characteristics
- Workflows, templates, and systems fully integrated  
- AI used for structured outputs  
- Clear decision logic applied consistently  

### AI‑Ops Indicators
- AI‑assisted risk detection  
- AI‑generated recommendations  

### Goals to Advance
- Introduce metrics framework  
- Automate operational reviews  
- Improve predictive capabilities  

---

## 6. Level 4 — Predictive AI‑Ops

### Characteristics
- AI predicts risks and priority shifts  
- Metrics tracked automatically  
- Cross‑team alignment monitored continuously  

### AI‑Ops Indicators
- Predictive modeling  
- Automated insights  
- Early‑warning signals  

### Goals to Advance
- Introduce autonomous workflows  
- Strengthen governance  
- Expand automation  

---

## 7. Level 5 — Autonomous Operations

### Characteristics
- AI executes workflows autonomously  
- Human oversight focused on exceptions  
- Operational decisions optimized continuously  

### AI‑Ops Indicators
- Autonomous decision loops  
- Self‑correcting processes  
- Fully integrated governance  

### Goals to Maintain
- Continuous improvement  
- Governance enforcement  
- Ethical and compliant AI usage  

---

## 8. Progression Path

Organizations typically progress:

**Ad‑Hoc → Structured → Integrated → Predictive → Autonomous**

Each stage increases:

- Clarity  
- Velocity  
- Alignment  
- Reliability  
- AI‑assisted capability  

---

## 9. Usage

The maturity model is used for:

- Operational assessments  
- Strategic planning  
- AI‑Ops adoption roadmaps  
- Executive reporting  
- Team enablement  

---

## 10. Summary

The AI‑Ops Maturity Model provides:

- A clear roadmap for operational evolution  
- Defined stages of AI‑Ops adoption  
- Guidance for improving clarity, velocity, and alignment  
- A foundation for long‑term operational excellence
