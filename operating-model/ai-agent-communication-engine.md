# AI Agent Communication Engine

A structured communication model defining how AI agents communicate, summarize, escalate, and deliver information with clarity, consistency, alignment, and compliance within the AI‑Ops Framework.

---

## 1. Purpose

To ensure all AI agent communication is:

- Clear  
- Structured  
- Aligned  
- Risk‑aware  
- Persona‑accurate  
- Cadence‑compliant  
- Stakeholder‑appropriate  
- Audit‑ready  
- Compliance‑safe  

The Communication Engine defines the rules and structures for all agent communication.

---

## 2. Communication Principles

AI agent communication is based on eight core principles:

1. **Clarity** — Communication must be unambiguous and easy to understand.  
2. **Structure** — All communication must follow structured templates.  
3. **Persona Accuracy** — Tone must match the loaded persona.  
4. **Cadence Compliance** — Communication timing must follow cadence rules.  
5. **Alignment Awareness** — Communication must reflect alignment scoring.  
6. **Risk Awareness** — Risks must be surfaced early and clearly.  
7. **Stakeholder Fit** — Communication must match the audience.  
8. **Auditability** — Communication must be traceable and logged.

---

## 3. Communication Structure

All communication must include:

- Summary  
- Context  
- Key points  
- Risks  
- Dependencies  
- Alignment score  
- Recommended actions  
- Stakeholder‑ready summary  
- Compliance notes  

This structure ensures consistency and auditability.

---

## 4. Communication Types

AI agents must support six communication types:

### **A. Status Communication**
- Weekly updates  
- Sprint updates  
- Roadmap updates  

### **B. Risk Communication**
- Risk reviews  
- Escalation reports  
- Risk alerts  

### **C. Decision Communication**
- Decision records  
- Tradeoff summaries  
- Rationale explanations  

### **D. Dependency Communication**
- Dependency reviews  
- Readiness updates  
- Blocker alerts  

### **E. Alignment Communication**
- Alignment checks  
- Drift detection summaries  

### **F. Stakeholder Communication**
- Leadership summaries  
- Team summaries  
- Cross‑team updates  

---

## 5. Persona Enforcement

Communication must:

- Match persona tone  
- Match persona vocabulary  
- Match persona structure  
- Avoid persona drift  

Persona violations are classified as **communication drift**.

---

## 6. Cadence Enforcement

AI agents must follow:

- Weekly cadence  
- Sprint cadence  
- Escalation cadence  
- Decision cadence  
- Risk cadence  

Cadence violations are **high‑severity** when they impact delivery.

---

## 7. Alignment‑Aware Communication

All communication must include:

- Alignment score  
- Alignment rationale  
- Drift indicators  
- Priority fit  
- Roadmap context  

Alignment score < 0.5 must trigger escalation.

---

## 8. Risk‑Aware Communication

All communication must:

- Surface risks early  
- Classify severity  
- Provide mitigation steps  
- Identify blockers  
- Recommend escalation when needed  

High‑severity risks must be highlighted prominently.

---

## 9. Stakeholder‑Specific Communication Rules

### **Leadership**
- High‑level  
- Risk‑focused  
- Alignment‑focused  
- Clear next steps  

### **Teams**
- Detailed  
- Execution‑focused  
- Dependency‑focused  

### **Cross‑Team**
- Coordination‑focused  
- Timeline‑focused  
- Blocker‑focused  

### **Compliance**
- Redaction‑focused  
- Classification‑focused  
- Audit‑ready  

---

## 10. Escalation Communication Rules

Escalation communication must include:

- Triggering condition  
- Severity  
- Immediate impact  
- Required actions  
- Urgency level  
- Stakeholder summary  

Escalations must use the Escalation Report Template.

---

## 11. Compliance‑Aware Communication

Communication must:

- Redact sensitive data  
- Include classification labels  
- Include compliance notes  
- Follow redaction rules  
- Follow audit rules  

Compliance violations are **high‑severity**.

---

## 12. Audit Requirements

All communication must be logged with:

- Timestamp  
- Actor  
- Inputs used  
- Outputs generated  
- Alignment score  
- Risks identified  
- Compliance notes  
- Classification level

The AI Agent Communication Engine defines how AI agents communicate clearly, consistently, safely, and in alignment with organizational priorities, risk posture, and compliance requirements.
