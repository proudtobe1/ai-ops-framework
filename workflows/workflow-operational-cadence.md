# Operational Cadence Workflow

A structured weekly operating rhythm that ensures clarity, alignment, and velocity across teams.

---

## Purpose

To create a predictable, AI‑supported cadence that keeps teams aligned, reduces ambiguity, and accelerates execution.

---

## Weekly Cadence Overview

### Monday — Alignment
- Review priorities  
- AI‑generated summaries of ongoing work  
- Identify blockers  
- Confirm ownership  

### Wednesday — Progress Check
- Mid‑week status review  
- AI‑assisted risk detection  
- Adjust priorities if needed  

### Friday — Outcomes
- Review completed work  
- AI‑generated weekly report  
- Capture learnings  
- Plan next week  

---

## Inputs
- Team updates  
- Project data  
- AI‑generated summaries  
- Risk signals  

---

## Outputs
- Weekly alignment  
- Clear priorities  
- Updated risks  
- Documented progress  
- AI‑generated weekly report  

---

## AI‑Assisted Components
- Summarization  
- Prioritization  
- Risk detection  
- Communication templates
