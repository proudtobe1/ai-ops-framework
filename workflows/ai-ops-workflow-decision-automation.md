# AI‑Assisted Decision Workflow

This workflow provides a structured approach for using AI to support operational and strategic decisions.

## Purpose

To help operators, Chiefs of Staff, and cross‑functional leaders make faster, clearer, and more consistent decisions using AI‑enabled inputs.

## Workflow Steps

1. **Define the decision**  
   Clarify the scope, constraints, and required outcome.

2. **Gather inputs**  
   Collect relevant data, context, and stakeholder perspectives.

3. **Generate AI‑assisted analysis**  
   Use AI tools to summarize, compare options, or highlight risks.

4. **Evaluate options**  
   Compare alternatives using decision criteria.

5. **Make the decision**  
   Select the best option based on evidence and alignment with goals.

6. **Communicate the decision**  
   Share the outcome, rationale, and next steps.

7. **Review and refine**  
   After execution, evaluate the decision’s impact and adjust the workflow as needed.

## Notes

This workflow will expand as the AI‑Ops Framework evolves.
